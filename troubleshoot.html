Buttons Not Responding Properly

Clean around the buttons using compressed air
Check for software updates on your Xbox or PC

Controller Not Connecting

Attempt re-syncing the controller by using the button on the controller and the Xbox / PC

Stick Drift

Calibrate the controller within the settings of your Xbox or PC
Try cleaning around the joysticks

Controller Not Turning On

If you are using a battery controller, replace the batteries
If you are using a wired controller, try using a different outlet for plugging your controller in
Try connecting with USB if the issue still persists

Headset Not Working

Make sure that the headset is fully plugged in. Unplug and re-plug in
Check the audio settings on your console or PC to make sure there is a sound output

Bluetooth Connection Issues

Remove the device from your Bluetooth settings. Then reconnect it.
Make sure the device you’re using is compatible with your Xbox controller
Update firmware for your controller if needed
