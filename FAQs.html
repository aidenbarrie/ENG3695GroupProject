How do you prevent stick drift?

Keep your controller in an open space where pressure isn’t being applied to either stick.

Are there Wired and Wireless Options?

Yes, both wired and wireless controllers exist for the platform.

How do you pair the device with the Xbox?

If wireless then you can connect using the pair button on the back of the controller and the front right side of the Xbox. Otherwise you can plug in the controller using a USB cable.

USB port doesn’t work?

Change cables and try to clean out the ports then see if it works. checkout the the troubleshooting page for more information.

Can I connect my controller to a PC?

You can easily plug in an Xbox controller to a PC for any game you might prefer a controller for.

What if its wireless?

Using a Microsoft Xbox Wireless Adapter you can easily connect your wireless controller to your PC.

How do I check my battery level?

You can check the battery level by pressing the home button, going to the main menu and looking at the battery icon in the top right corner.

What does the Share button do?

It allows you to take screenshots and record game play.

Are Xbox controllers compatible with other consoles?

Yes, Xbox one Controllers can be used on later consoles like Series X|S.

Can they be used on PlayStation or the Switch?

They are not compatible with competitor products.
