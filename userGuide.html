Controller Functions

To turn on your Xbox controller, press and hold the Xbox button (the large circular button with the Xbox logo in the center) until it lights up. If it does not turn on, ensure that the batteries are properly inserted or that the controller is charged if using a rechargeable battery pack.

Pairing your controller:
 1. Power on the Xbox console by pressing the Xbox button on the front.
2. Press and hold the pairing button on the console until the Xbox logo starts flashing.
3. On the controller, press and hold the pairing button (a small button located near the USB port) until the Xbox logo starts blinking (Indicated by red arrow).
4. Once connected, the Xbox logo will stay lit, indicating a successful pairing.

ABXY Buttons

A, B, X, Y Buttons – Used for actions in games, see game menu for specified action. Also used to open and back out of pages and games, A is open and B is back.

Joysticks and D-Pad

Used for navigation both in game and through menus. For navigation either D-pad or joystick can be used.

Bumpers and Triggers

-Triggers (LT & RT) – Used for aiming, acceleration, or other game-specific actions.
• Bumpers (LB & RB) – Used for secondary actions
